What is this?

Kaboom is a minesweeper variant made by Pawe≈Ç Marczewski which is "cruel, but fair":

  • The mines are not placed at the beginning, but determined as you play. There is no hidden state.
  • If you try to guess, the game will always choose the worst scenario.
  • Except when you are forced to guess (there are no safe cells anywhere). Then, guessing is completely safe.

Superchording is an extension to regular chording, which allows for describing complex logical deductions though clicking on numbers.

  • Left clicking on a number will include it in the deduction.
  • Right clicking anywhere on the board will reset the deduction.
  • Middle click to enter/exit "advanced mode".
  • By default, the game will not let you manually reveal any cells, unless you are forced to guess.
